USAGE:
  Print out a help message:
      pird -h
  List available devices, images in DIR or content of FILE:
      pird -l [DIR | FILE]
  Extract audio data from FILE:
      pird [OPTION...] FILE [-]
  Â 
OPTIONS:
  -j, --job, --jobs JOB[,...]
      what should be ripped, multiple JOBs are delimited by ,
  -f, --format FORMAT
      the audio format used for output: WAV (default) or PCM
  -t, --trackwise
      split ranges at track bounds
  -p, --paranoia
      rip in paranoia mode (FILE has to be an optical drive)
      NOTE: This feature isn't implemented yet!
  -v --verbose
      be verbose, each occurrence increases log level
  -q, --quiet
      be quiet
  -s --simulate
      simulate execution of jobs
